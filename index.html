What is Prenatal Pro?

Prenatal Pro is a full prenatal that comes as a few capsules a day, because you honestly cannot fit a complete prenatal into a single pill. Two things really make it different. First, the folate: it uses the methylated form, the one your body can use right away, which gets past the trouble some women have — especially those with the common MTHFR gene variation — converting plain folic acid. Second, the choline: most women do not get enough during pregnancy, it is important for the baby's brain, and a lot of prenatals leave it out entirely — this one keeps it in. On top of that, you get a gentle, well-absorbed iron for the extra blood your body makes while pregnant (and it is easier on your stomach than the rough kind), iodine for the baby's thyroid and brain, active B12 and other active B vitamins, plus vitamin D, vitamin C, zinc, and a good base of minerals. It comes from Designs for Health, a practitioner brand in Connecticut you mostly get through a clinician or their own store. Take it with food, and check your bottle for the exact numbers.
